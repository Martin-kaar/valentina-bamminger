BOARD

GAME

IT’S NOT THAT HARD
UNTIL THE RULES ARN’T
MADE FOR YOU.

“It’s not that hard” is a satirical board game that playfully
exposes structural inequality through reversed gender roles.

The aim of the game is to make social privileges and discrimination tangible not only intellectually, but also physically and emotionally.
