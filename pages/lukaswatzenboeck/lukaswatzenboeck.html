BRANDBOOK

A Brandbook is a visual summary that brings together all the essential elements of a brand in one cohesive document. It explains the logo in detail - its construction, meaning, and use, while also outlining the color palette, typography, and imagery.  Beyond technical rules, it conveys the personality of the brand, creating a clear and inspiring reference that helps maintain consistency and allows anyone to truly understand and feel the brand.
